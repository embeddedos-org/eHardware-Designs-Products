SPECIAL FABRICATION NOTES
─────────────────────────
  1. Layers 1-2 MUST use Rogers RO4003C — standard FR4 has 4 dB excess loss
     at Ka-band (34 GHz) which halves detection range.
  2. SIW via fence on Layer 1-2: 0.3mm drill, 0.5mm pitch, forming waveguide
     walls for the phased-array feed network.
  3. Via-in-pad with VIPPO (Via In Pad Plated Over) required for U1, U2 (AWR2944),
     U3 (RK3588S), U4 (TEKTON3) BGA fan-out.
  4. Impedance test coupons required on each panel — 50Ω, 100Ω, 120Ω.
  5. RF section (layers 1-2) physically separated from digital section with
     ground via stitching fence at 1mm pitch.
  6. Thermal vias under U3 (RK3588S): 5×5 array, 0.3mm drill, 1mm pitch,
     connected to exposed pad on bottom side.
  7. Edge-plated castellations for shielding can attachment (RF section).
  8. Black solder mask required for display contrast and thermal absorption.
