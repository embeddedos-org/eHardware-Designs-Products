# Kinetic Regeneration Subsystem

## Regeneration Sources

| Source | Vehicle(s) | Method | Recovery |
|--------|-----------|--------|----------|
| **Wheel braking** | Eco Car | Motor as generator during deceleration | Up to 80 kW |
| **Rotor descent** | Urban Drone, Combo | Rotors as generators during controlled descent | Up to 200 kW |
| **Glide descent** | Space Shuttle, Combo | Drag-to-electric via body-mounted generators | Up to 50 kW |
| **Atmospheric re-entry** | Space Shuttle, Combo | Thermal-to-electric via thermoelectric generators | Up to 5 kW |
