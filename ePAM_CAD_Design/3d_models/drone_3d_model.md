# Urban Drone — 3D Model Specifications

## Exterior Dimensions
- Length: 7.5m, Wingspan: 12m (extended) / 4m (folded), Height: 2.8m
- Body: Tear-drop fuselage with gull-wing doors
- Rotors: 8x 1.5m props on tilt nacelles (4 front, 4 rear)
- Solar skin: Upper wing + roof surfaces (18m2 perovskite)

## Materials
- Fuselage: Carbon-fiber monocoque
- Rotor arms: Carbon-fiber tubular (retractable)
- Canopy: Electrochromic glass
- Landing gear: Carbon-fiber tricycle (retractable)

## Interior
- 4 seats: 2+2 configuration, carbon-fiber bucket seats
- Panoramic canopy
- 15" central touchscreen
- Ambient lighting
- 150L cargo behind rear seats
