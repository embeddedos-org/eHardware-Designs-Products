# Eco Car — 3D Model Specifications

## Exterior Dimensions
- Length: 4.7m, Width: 1.85m, Height: 1.45m (sedan) / 1.65m (crossover)
- Body: Aerodynamic sedan or crossover form (Cd 0.21)
- Solar panels: Roof, hood, trunk lid (6m2 integrated perovskite)

## Materials
- Body: Recycled aluminum frame + 3D-printed polymer panels
- Glass: Low-E double-pane with solar coating
- Underbody: Flat composite aero panel
- Wheels: 19" aero-optimized (drag-reducing covers)

## Interior
- 4-5 seats: 2+3 configuration
- 15" center display + HUD
- Sustainable materials: recycled ocean plastic, plant-based leather
- 450L trunk
- Flat floor (battery under floor)
