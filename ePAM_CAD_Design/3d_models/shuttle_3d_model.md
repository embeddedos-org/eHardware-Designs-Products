# Space Shuttle — 3D Model Specifications

## Exterior Dimensions
- Length: 14m, Wingspan: 9m (delta wing), Height: 4.5m
- Body: Lifting body with delta wings
- TPS: Ceramic thermal protection tiles on windward surfaces
- Windows: Triple-pane fused silica (1650C rated)

## Materials
- Primary structure: Ti-6Al-4V (titanium alloy)
- Skin: Carbon-fiber composite + aerogel insulation
- TPS tiles: Silicon carbide ceramic
- Deployable solar: GaAs panels on articulating booms
- Inflatable concentrator: Aluminized Mylar (5m diameter)

## Interior
- 4 seats: 1 pilot + 3 passengers, reclining for G-force
- Observation windows: 4x large side windows + 1 overhead
- Life support console
- Emergency equipment bay
