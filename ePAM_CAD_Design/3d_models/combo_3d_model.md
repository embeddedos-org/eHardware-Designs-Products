# Combo Unit — 3D Model Specifications

## Exterior Dimensions
- Length: 11m, Wingspan: 10m (rotors) / 6m (wings only), Height: 3.5m
- Body: Blended wing-body with retractable rotor nacelles
- Dual-mode surfaces: Perovskite solar (atmospheric) + deployable GaAs (space)

## Materials
- Frame: Ti-6Al-4V skeleton
- Skin: Carbon-fiber/aerogel composite
- TPS zones: Ceramic tiles on nose + leading edges + belly
- Rotor housing: Retractable fairings (flush when stowed)

## Trans-Atmospheric Features
- Rotors retract into body above 500m for reduced drag
- Wing morphing: sweep angle adjusts for subsonic/supersonic
- Thermal management: Active cooling channels in TPS zones
- Deployable solar: 30m2 GaAs panels unfurl in space

## Interior
- 4 seats: pressure-sealed cabin, G-rated
- Multi-mode display: flight info (drone) / orbital track (space)
- Emergency: dual BPS + ejection seat option
