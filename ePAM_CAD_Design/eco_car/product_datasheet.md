# Eco Car — Solar-Hybrid Ground Vehicle Product Datasheet

## Overview

The Eco Car is a mass-market 4-5 seat ground vehicle with solar-hybrid power. The vehicle surface is coated in perovskite solar cells, supplemented by a solid-state battery and optional hydrogen fuel cell range extender. Near-zero operating cost.

## Power System

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Solar panels** | 6m2 integrated perovskite cells (roof, hood, trunk) |
| **Solar efficiency** | 25% (perovskite/silicon tandem) |
| **Solar output** | 1.5 kW peak, ~800W average |
| **Daily solar harvest** | 4-6 kWh (8hr sun exposure) |
| **Battery** | 75 kWh solid-state lithium |
| **Battery weight** | 150 kg (500 Wh/kg) |
| **Motor** | Dual permanent magnet synchronous (AWD) |
| **Motor power** | 200 kW (268 hp) combined |
| **H2 fuel cell (optional)** | 30 kW PEM range extender + 3 kg H2 tank |
| **Regen braking** | Up to 80 kW recapture |
| **V2G** | Bidirectional charging — sell power back to grid |
