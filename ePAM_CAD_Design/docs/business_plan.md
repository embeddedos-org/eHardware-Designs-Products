# ePAM — Business Plan

## Multi-Tier Revenue Model

### A. Direct Vehicle Sales

| Vehicle | Price Range | Margin | Year 1 Target |
|---------|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Eco Car | $28K-$45K | 25-30% | 10,000 units |
| Urban Drone | $85K-$120K | 30-35% | 500 units |
| Space Shuttle | $2M-$4M | 40% | 5 units |
| Combo Unit | $5M-$9M | 45% | 2 units |

### B. Subscription Air Mobility (Drone Fleet)

| Plan | Monthly Fee | Flights/Month | Target Users |
|------|------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Commuter** | $499/mo | 20 flights (within city) | Urban professionals |
| **Regional** | $999/mo | 10 flights (city-to-city, 300km) | Business travelers |
| **Premium** | $2,499/mo | Unlimited local + 5 regional | Executives |

### C. Fractional Ownership (Space)

| Share | Price | Block Hours/Year | Target |
|-------|-------|-----------------|--------|
| 1/8th shuttle | $350K | 50 hours | HNWI |
| 1/16th combo | $400K | 25 hours | HNWI |

### D. Ancillary Revenue

| Stream | Revenue Model |
|--------|--------------|
| Software / OTA updates | $29/month subscription per vehicle |
| Energy resale (V2G) | Revenue share — vehicle sells power to grid |
| Fleet leasing (B2B) | Leasing to ride-share operators |
| Space tourism tickets | $250K per seat per flight |
| Franchise / licensing | License technology to regional operators |
| Gov / Defense contracts | Custom variants for military / emergency services |

## Cost Efficiency Levers

| Lever | Impact |
|-------|--------|
| Shared platform chassis | -20% per-vehicle engineering cost |
| Modular power packs | Same battery/FC across all 4 vehicles |
| 3D-printed body panels | -40% tooling cost vs stamping |
| OTA software updates | Continuous feature upgrades, no recalls |
| Vertical solar cell manufacturing | In-house perovskite production |
| Battery swap network | Faster turnaround, shared inventory |
