# Future Designs — Donor Showcase

These directories are **concept-stage placeholders** for hardware
products the EmbeddedOS organisation intends to develop next. Each one
exists today as a datasheet stub, a BOM placeholder, and a one-page
business plan — enough for a donor to browse, evaluate, and sponsor.

> Designs in this folder do **not** count toward the canonical
> 13-product roster. They graduate (and move to a top-level directory
> under [`eCAD-Hardware-Products`](https://github.com/embeddedos-org/eCAD-Hardware-Products))
> only after they have a working KiCad schematic, a real BOM, and an
> integration smoke test.
